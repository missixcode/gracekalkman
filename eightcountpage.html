EIGHT COUNT APP

Summary

Mission

The mission behind designing the Eight Count app is to provide an invigorating, dynamic, and motivating fitness solution for those who want to train like a boxer but prefer to work out at home. Aimed at users who may feel too shy, too busy, or too financially constrained to attend a boxing gym, Eight Count offers a convenient and accessible way to learn boxing and get fit in the comfort of their own space. The app features instructional videos that guide users through boxing and fitness movements, making it easy to follow along at their own pace. Users can set personalized goals, whether it's to lose weight or build muscle, and progress through different skill levels as they watch and learn. The app is designed to keep users engaged and empowered, helping them achieve their fitness goals with confidence and energy.

Client

  • Eight Count, LLC.
  • Chicago, IL

My Contribution

As a UI/UX Designer for the Eight Count app, I helped shape the app’s user experience from start to finish. I began by creating a moodboard based on research to set the visual style and feel of the app. I then designed a pattern library to keep the design consistent throughout. I also created a sitemap to plan the app’s structure and user flow, followed by both low-fidelity and high-fidelity wireframes to visualize how users would interact with the app. These steps helped ensure the app was visually appealing and easy to use.

Process

Discovery

During user testing, several key insights were gathered that significantly influenced the design and functionality of the platform. Users emphasized the importance of a streamlined, intuitive navigation experience, particularly for searching and filtering properties, suggesting that the search functionality needed to be even more refined for quick access to high-end listings. Many users appreciated the interactive 3D tours but requested clearer instructions on how to fully navigate and interact with the virtual tours. Additionally, feedback highlighted the desire for a more personalized experience, with users seeking tailored recommendations and more in-depth property details right at their fingertips. Based on these findings, we made enhancements to improve ease of use, visibility of interactive elements, and integrated more personalized features, ensuring a seamless, luxurious browsing experience.

Planning/Structure

After conducting thorough user research, I moved on to creating the sitemap and wireframes to translate the insights gained into a clear and structured plan for the website's layout and functionality. The research helped identify key user needs and pain points, allowing me to prioritize essential features and determine the most intuitive flow for users. By developing the sitemap, I could establish a solid foundation for the website’s structure, ensuring easy navigation and accessibility to important sections. The wireframes then allowed me to visually represent the user interface, ensuring the design was aligned with the goals of enhancing user experience, functionality, and accessibility.

Web/UI/UX

In the Web Design and UI/UX design phase, I made deliberate choices to create a visually appealing, user-friendly platform that exudes luxury and sophistication. I chose dark teal and gold as accent colors for buttons to evoke a sense of elegance and exclusivity. The majority of the website is white to create a clean, minimalistic background that allows the properties themselves to take center stage. I opted for cards to display the homes because they offer a visually organized and efficient way to present essential property details at a glance. This allows users to quickly assess key information, such as price, location, and size, before diving deeper into individual property pages. The overall minimal design was intentionally chosen to maintain a luxurious and refined feel. A clean, uncluttered interface creates a sense of sophistication, ensuring that users experience the website as both modern and high-end.

User Testing & Redesign

During user testing, feedback indicated that the Agent Finder feature was not as intuitive or widely used as anticipated, and users expressed a preference for a more streamlined experience. As a result, we decided to remove the Agent Finder feature from the platform. Additionally, we also removed the Home Loans section, as it was not as relevant to the core needs of our target audience. By eliminating these sections, we were able to simplify the site’s navigation, focusing more on the key features that users found most valuable. This allowed for a cleaner, more intuitive interface that enhanced the overall user experience.
